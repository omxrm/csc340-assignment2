About This Gallery

Welcome to the UNCG Cat Gallery! The website was created as a fun and interesting way for Spartans to present and celebrate the most adorable cats on and around campus. It's a space where students and guests can browse cute images and learn more about each presented pet.

Technically, this site shows you how you can utilize semantic HTML and Bootstrap 5 to develop a responsive user-centered project. It incorporates functionality such as a search box, a detail page for each of the cats, and a form for inputting new entries.

If you are visiting Cora the Russian Blue, Mocha the Siamese, or just visiting us in general, we hope our gallery put a grin on your face. We hope you stopped on by!
